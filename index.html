About us

The Acoustery solution is fully automated and resolves the drawbacks of existing cough analysis systems. Acoustery uses state-of-the-art machine learning-based approaches to analyze text and audio data. The principle of the system is to apply the best modern clinical practices and adapt them for convenient use.

Why is it time for respiratory disease sound analysis solutions?
TimeThe COVID-19 pandemiс made distant and preventive diagnostics a very hot topic. This niche will only grow with time.
Availability of tests Swab test and antibody test are either invasive or need specific infrastructure while acoustic diagnostics only require your mobile phone.
IsolationIsolation will remain widespread in many countries even after the COVID-19 pandemic. This will require distant diagnostics.

Business implementations
01
Drug Store Kiosks

Most drug store staff has problems with customers who ask them about their cough. Kiosks with microphones may solve this inconvenience.

Business implementations
02
Touchless ATMs

Most ATMs have embedded microphones to detect what happens around. They may be used for acoustic monitoring to adjust the data on disease spreading and help the municipalities.

Business implementations
03
Touchless parking and airport terminals

Touchless terminals with a voice control function may be used to detect possible diseases of those who want to go on board or to the shopping mall.

Business implementations
04
Vending terminals

Vending terminals with a voice control function may be used to evaluate the risk of contagious disease spreading.

Business implementations
05
Public transport

Public microphones to detect the cough indicating a socially dangerous disease. The cough may be detected even in noisy surroundings.

Business implementations
06
Call-centers

Call-centres need an objective third party opinion on those who call to report on their health issues.

Insurance

Insurance companies need to track health conditions of people who work in hazardous industries, like truck drivers in the mining industry, for example. The tracking may be performed with a microphone in the truck cabin.
